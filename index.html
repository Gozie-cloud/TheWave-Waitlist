Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the Starlink hotspot business?

The Starlink hotspot business involves using Starlink internet together with networking equipment to distribute internet access, manage users, control data usage, and sell internet bundles to customers.

How much do I need to start?

Startup costs typically range between ₦2.5M–₦3M depending on your location, power setup, and equipment requirements.

How much can I make monthly?

Profitability varies based on location, demand, pricing, competition, and execution. Many operators target monthly profits ranging from ₦300k–₦600k+.

Do I need technical experience?

No. The consultation is designed for both beginners and technically experienced individuals.

Is my location suitable?

Location is one of the biggest factors in the success of the business. During the consultation, I'll explain what makes a location suitable and how to assess yours.

How does the system sell data bundles?

The setup uses networking and management tools that allow operators to create bundles, manage users, authorize access, and control usage. The full process is explained during the consultation.

What equipment is required?

The business requires Starlink together with supporting networking equipment and power infrastructure. A complete breakdown is provided during the consultation.

Can I run the business without solar?

This depends on your area's power reliability. Power requirements and available options are discussed during the consultation.

How many users can one setup handle?

Capacity depends on the setup design, demand, and network configuration. This is covered in detail during the consultation.

What coverage can I expect?

Coverage depends on location, terrain, obstructions, installation quality, and equipment configuration. There is no universal answer, which is why location assessment is important.

  • Buying unsuitable equipment
  • Poor power planning
  • Incorrect setup and configuration
  • Underestimating demand

These mistakes can be expensive and are discussed during the consultation.
